description: LoRa1278 is a compact wireless transceiver module based on the Semtech SX1278 chip, supporting LoRa, FSK, GFSK, and OOK modulation. It offers high sensitivity (-139 dBm), a maximum output power of 20 dBm, and operates over a customizable frequency range of 137–1020 MHz. The module features low power consumption (10.8 mA RX, 100 mA TX @20 dBm, <0.2 µA sleep) and a wide operating temperature range of -40 °C to +85 °C.
