description: The L293D Motor Shield is a great motor controller for Arduino. It controls servos, steppers and DC motors with an Arduino. The shield uses the L293D chipset to provide 0.6A per bridge (1.2A peak) with thermal shutdown protection and internal kickback protection diodes. It can run motors from 4.5V to 25V and supports up to 4 bi-directional DC motors, 2 servos, or 2 stepper motors.
