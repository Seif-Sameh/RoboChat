description: ESP-WROOM-32 is a compact Wi‑Fi, Bluetooth Classic, and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) module based on the ESP32-D0WDQ6 chip. It features dual Xtensa LX6 cores, 520 KB on‑chip SRAM, 4 MB external SPI flash, and a rich set of peripherals including capacitive touch, ADC, DAC, UART, I2C, SPI, I2S, SDIO, Ethernet MAC, and more. Designed for low‑power sensor networks, voice encoding, music streaming, and MP3 decoding.
