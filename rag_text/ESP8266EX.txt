description: ESP8266EX is a highly integrated Wi‑Fi SoC that supports 802.11b/g/n with up to 72.2 Mbps data rate. It features a Tensilica L106 processor, on‑chip SRAM, and a rich set of interfaces (UART, SPI, SDIO, I2C, I2S, PWM, IR). The module is designed for low power consumption, compact QFN32 packaging, and versatile application in IoT devices.
